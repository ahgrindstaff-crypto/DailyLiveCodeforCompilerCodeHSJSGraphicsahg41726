Class Workflow

This is real-time logic practice with small programs. Lead in with weekly focus on each of the Four Pillars of Computational Thinking.

Directions for students: "Once we write it together, you will need to change it around to something you like. Once you have something you are proud of, take a screenshot with Command+Shift+4 and click/drag across the area you want saved. Post your screenshot on your Google Site website and submit the screenshot in Canvas for a grade."

"You will be using a JavaScript Graphics Console (IDE) to build and observe these programs. Your code will come to life. To make this easier, use your CodeHS Sandbox. To prevent cheating, it is best to live code these programs — you type and they type, with the opportunity for them to screenshot only once they successfully construct the code."

Students take screenshots for everything they do and place (with a title and/or description) on their ongoing Google Site website, then submit in Canvas for points.

Keeping a library like this in CodeHS Sandbox lets us share and access anytime, any year.
